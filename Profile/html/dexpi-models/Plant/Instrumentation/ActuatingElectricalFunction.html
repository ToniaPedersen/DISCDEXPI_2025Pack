ActuatingElectricalFunction¶

Plant::Instrumentation::ActuatingElectricalFunction

Overview¶

Concrete Class

RDL: ACTUATING ELECTRICAL FUNCTION (http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/ActuatingElectricalFunction)

An actuation setting electrical function. It covers all types of electrical consumers, e.g., motors and heaters.
