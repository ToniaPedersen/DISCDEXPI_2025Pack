Plant::ProcessEquipment::Agitator

Overview¶

Concrete Class

RDL: AGITATOR (http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS16045622)

An Agitator is a dynamic mixer that stirs or shakes fluids by reaction force from moving vanes.
